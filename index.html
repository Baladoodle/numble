house rules

How it works

  1. Pick a mode: 3, 4, or 5–digit. The materials, target range, and timer scale with the mode. Cycle with D.
  2. Materials are drawn in order, each tumbling quickly. Press space to lock.
  3. Once the last material locks, a target is computed and rolled — always one your materials can solve.
  4. Compose an expression from the materials and basic arithmetic. Allowed operators: + − × ÷ ( ) — every intermediate result must be an integer, no fractions.
  5. Use any subset of the materials, each at most once.
  6. Hit enter to submit, or let the clock run out.
  7. Practice: the running sum is shown while you type — for learning the feel. Practice games don't count toward your stats. Toggle with P.

Seed

A seed is a phrase that pins the game — same seed + same round = same materials and target. Two players sharing a seed race on identical boards. Seeded games don't count toward your stats.
